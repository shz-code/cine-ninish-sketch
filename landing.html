CineQuiz Showdown

Where Movie Magic Meets Creative Brilliance!

Know More

The competition that transforms your love for movies and series into a thrilling journey of knowledge and creativity.

Our mantra is simple: 'Watch,Write & Win,' inviting participants to embark on a cinematic adventure like never before.

CineQuiz

Competition Structure:

1

Online Registration

To participate, you need to register online. There are two segments – 'Cine Quiz' and 'Cine Story Writing.' You can participate in either or both segments. The registration fee is 250 taka for each segment or both.

2

Online Exam

For the 'Cine Quiz' segment, during registration, you will choose your preferred movie/series bundle. There will be 40 questions related to your chosen content. You need to select the correct answers and submit them. For 'Cine Story Writing,' participants need to write a cinema-worthy story and submit it as a PDF. Deadline for Quiz and Story Submission: February 2, 2024.

3

Preliminary Selection

The top 200 participants with the highest scores from the 'Cine Quiz' segment and the top 10 storytellers will directly proceed to the gala round.

4

Gala Round

his round will take place at a designated venue. Three winners from the 'Cine Quiz' segment and three from 'Cine Story Writing' will be announced, each receiving a cash prize of 4 lakhs taka, a certificate, an award, a celebrity meet-up, a Cox's Bazar-Saint Martin 3-day-2-night trip, and additional attractive prizes from the main sponsor.

Event Highlights

Cine Quiz: Lights, Camera, Trivia!

How it Unfolds: Dive into the world of film and series as you watch, learn, and conquer the Cine Quiz. From classic quotes to behind-the-scenes mysteries, every frame holds a clue to your victory.

Watch & Win: Crack the code to cinephile glory! Every correct answer not only brings you closer to being crowned the 'Cine Quiz Champion' but also unlocks the door to exclusive awards, celebrity meet-ups, and a special tour of cinematic landmarks.

Cine Story Writing: Script Your Cinematic Tale!

Lights, Camera, Imagination: Become the storyteller you were born to be! Pen down your original script, crafting a narrative that could grace the silver screen. Your story might just be the next blockbuster in the making.

From Pen to Prize: The winning story doesn't just earn accolades – it opens doors to a world of possibilities, including prize money, a chance to meet industry luminaries, and the thrill of seeing your creation come to life.
